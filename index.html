Operation Directives

Rules & Eligibility

Eligibility

  • Open to all UG/PG students with a valid college identity card.
  • Teams must consist of 2–4 members. Inter-specialization teams allowed.
  • Registration via Unstop platform is mandatory before the deadline.

Hackathon Rules

  • Work must be built entirely during the 24-hour period. No pre-built modules.
  • Plagiarism or unfair means result in immediate disqualification.
  • Participants must bring their own hardware: laptops, extension boards, etc.

Perks & Facilities

  • Fuel for the mission: Breakfast and Lunch will be provided for all hacks.
  • Accommodation support is available (Contact Head Organizer individually).
  • Official participation certificates and exciting goodies for every team.

Note: Registrations close 1 week before the event. Slots are limited.
